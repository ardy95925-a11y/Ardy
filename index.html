The Operator is a specialized tactical unit focused on rapid-fire engagement and hidden threat detection. Unlike the standard Shooter, the Operator uses an SMG with high fire rates but lower per-bullet damage.

Overview

The Operator is essential for mid-game waves where stealth-based enemies like the Shadow Stalker begin to appear. Their high fire rate makes them excellent at handling crowds of weak enemies.
